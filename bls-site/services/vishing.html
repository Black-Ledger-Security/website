Voice Social Engineering

Vishing Campaigns

Voice-based social engineering targeting your personnel with custom pretexts tailored to your industry and organizational structure.

What We Test

We develop custom pretexts tailored to your industry and organizational structure, then execute controlled calling campaigns designed to test security awareness, credential handling, and information disclosure over the phone.

Vishing remains one of the most effective initial access vectors in real-world intrusions. Our campaigns measure how your employees respond under pressure, identify the specific types of information most commonly disclosed, and provide data-driven recommendations for awareness training improvements.

Campaign Structure

Every campaign begins with OSINT collection to develop realistic pretexts that mirror real-world attack scenarios. We build caller profiles, prepare scripts with escalation paths, and configure infrastructure to present convincing caller IDs.

During execution, we call targeted personnel using carefully developed scenarios. Common pretexts include IT support, vendor inquiries, executive impersonation, and compliance-related requests. Every call is documented with detailed notes on employee responses.

What You Receive

Detailed metrics on call completion rates, information disclosure rates, and credential compromise rates. Individual call summaries documenting what information was obtained and how. Statistical analysis identifying your most vulnerable departments and roles. Tailored recommendations for awareness training improvements based on the specific failure patterns we observe.
