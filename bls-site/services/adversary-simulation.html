Adversary Simulation

Our most comprehensive offering. Custom-built operations that execute the full attack chain against your organization across every available attack vector.

The Complete Attack Chain

Every operation is custom-built around your organization's specific threat profile and executes the full attack chain: a detailed threat profile report delivered to the client, extensive OSINT and onsite reconnaissance, vishing campaigns, targeted spear phishing, AI deepfake social engineering, physical breach attempts, and network implant deployment. If network access is achieved, Black Ledger proceeds with 24 to 48 hours of internal penetration testing leveraging the access gained through the implant.

No two engagements look the same. Operations begin with extensive physical and digital surveillance and advanced pretext development tailored to your industry, your facilities, and your people.

Operational Phases

Phase one delivers a comprehensive threat profile and OSINT assessment documenting your organization's full external exposure. Phase two develops and executes social engineering campaigns across voice, email, and AI-enabled vectors. Phase three conducts physical breach operations against your facilities. Phase four, if access is achieved, executes internal network penetration testing through deployed implants.

Who This Is For

These engagements are designed for organizations that want to understand exactly how a motivated, resourced adversary would target them across every available attack vector. Adversary simulation is our most comprehensive service and is built for organizations with mature security programs that need to test their detection and response capabilities against a persistent, adaptive threat.

What You Receive

A comprehensive operation report documenting the full attack narrative from initial reconnaissance through final objectives. Detailed findings across every domain tested: OSINT, social engineering, physical security, and network security. Executive briefing suitable for board-level presentation. Prioritized remediation roadmap addressing vulnerabilities across your entire security program.
