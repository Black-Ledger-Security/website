Intelligence Collection

OSINT & Threat Profiling

We show you what an adversary already knows about your organization through comprehensive digital footprint analysis and executive threat profiling.

What We Discover

Comprehensive digital footprint analysis that shows you what an adversary already knows about your organization. We map your external exposure across public records, social media, code repositories, data breaches, and the dark web to build a complete threat profile.

Executive threat profiling identifies specific individuals within your organization who present elevated risk due to their digital exposure, public visibility, or access to sensitive systems. Every finding is actionable: we tell you what is exposed, where it is exposed, and how to reduce it.

Collection Scope

Public records and corporate filings. Social media exposure across all platforms. Code repositories and technical infrastructure leaks. Data breach and credential exposure assessment. Dark web monitoring and threat actor mentions. Domain and subdomain enumeration. Employee enumeration and organizational mapping. Physical location intelligence. Vendor and third-party relationship mapping.

Executive Threat Profiling

High-value individuals within your organization are assessed individually for their digital exposure, personal security posture, and susceptibility to targeted social engineering. We identify the specific information an adversary would use to target them and provide individualized risk reduction recommendations.

What You Receive

A complete threat profile report documenting all discovered exposure with source attribution. Risk-scored findings organized by severity and exploitability. Specific remediation steps for each finding. Executive-level summary suitable for board or leadership briefings.
